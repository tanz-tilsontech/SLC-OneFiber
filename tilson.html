Welcome to the Tilson DataMap!

The Tilson DataMap is a tool for viewing and analyzing real-time data collected. The list below is data imported from Tilson Tecnhology. All data imported is property of Tilson Technology.

  • Demand Points

    • Locations where Fiber Cables Connect to the Equipment.

  • Construction Routes

    • Fulcrum Collected Data for Construction on Underground & Aerial Routes

  • Construction Resto

    • Fulcrum Collected Data for Construction Restoration Locations

  • Construction Hardscape

    • Fulcrum Collected Data for Construction Hardscape Locations

  • Engineered Structures

    • Engineered Data for Handoles

  • Engineered Routes

    • Engineered Data where Cable Pathways Break at Handholes and/or Poles

  • Engineered Segments

    • Engineered Data where Fiber Cables Break at Underground to Aerial Transitions or Splices

  • Engineered Sections

    • Engineered Data that Represent a Full Reel of Fiber Cable

  • Engineered Splices

    • Engineered Data where Fiber Cables Splice Together
