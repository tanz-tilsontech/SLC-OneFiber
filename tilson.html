Welcome to the Tilson DataMap!

ALL DATA SHOWN IS PROPERTY OF TILSON TECHNOLOGY

The Tilson DataMap is a tool for viewing and analyzing real-time data collected in the field pertaining Tilson Projects. Data has been collected in the field with 3GIS and Fulcrum.

  • Fulcrum Routes

    • Fulcrum Collected Data for Construction on Underground & Aerial Routes

  • Fulcrum Resto

    • Fulcrum Collected Data for Construction Restoration Locations

  • Fulcrum Hardscape

    • Fulcrum Collected Data for Construction Hardscape Locations

  • 3GIS Routes

    • Engineered Construction Routes that break at Handholes and/or Poles

  • 3GIS Segments

    • Engineered Fiber Segments that break at Underground to Aerial Transitions or Virtual Splices

  • 3GIS Sections

    • Engineered Fiber Routes that break at MCA or Reel-End Splice Locations

  • 3GIS Splices

    • Engineered Splice Closures that Splices Segments together


For questions related to the Tilson DataMap, contact us at E-Mail or visit our website @ TilsonTech.
